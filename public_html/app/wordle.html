Rules of Wordle Game

  1. Guess the five-letter word in six or fewer attempts.
  2. Each letter of the word is represented by a colored square.
  3. A green square means the letter is in the correct position.
  4. A yellow square means the letter is in the word, but not in the correct position.
  5. A gray square means the letter is not in the word.
